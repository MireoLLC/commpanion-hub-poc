Primary Care

Primary care on your terms.

A dedicated primary care provider and care team — for well visits, urgent care, messaging, prescriptions, and everyday support, all virtual-first.

Visit the full Primary Care site Call 833-PSH-CMPN

Overview

A relationship-based, virtual-first primary care experience.

Build a lasting relationship with a dedicated primary care provider and care team — without the waiting room. COMMpanion Primary Care brings well visits, urgent care, messaging, and everyday support into one convenient virtual experience.

What's included

Everything your everyday health needs.

  • Personalized healthcare plan

    Built around your history and goals.

  • Scheduled video visits

    Quality face-to-face time with your PCP.

  • Urgent care visits

    Mon–Fri, 7 a.m.–7 p.m.

  • Unlimited care-team messaging & calls

    Reach your team when you actually need them.

  • Prescription refills

    Renewals handled by your care team.

  • Referral & specialist navigation

    We help you get to the right place, fast.

How it works

From sign-up to ongoing support.

  1. Join

    Sign up as an individual or through your employer.

  2. Meet your PCP and care team

    Connect with a dedicated team that takes time to know you.

  3. Build your personalized health plan

    Shaped by your history, goals, and life.

  4. Get proactive, year-round support

    Visits, messaging, and follow-up that fit your schedule.

Who it's for

Individuals and employers who want relationship-based, convenient care.

If you've ever wished you could just message your doctor and actually get a person back, COMMpanion Primary Care was built for you — and for the organizations that want this kind of care for their people.
